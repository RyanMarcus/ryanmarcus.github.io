Six years of experience working with high performance computing technologies like GPGPUs and large scientific code-bases on state-of-the-art supercomputers. My work has included optimizing 3D reconstruction codes, designing accelerated Monte Carlo neutronics models, and working to gain a deep and scientific understanding of large parallel codes and the machines they run on.
