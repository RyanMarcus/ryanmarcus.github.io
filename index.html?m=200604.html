Luxer Update! Beta 9

2 Replies

I released a new version of Luxer today… Beta #9! There where not many changes in this release, but the goal of this release was to show I am still making Luxer and that it has been brought back.

Changes:
– Luxer disconnects when the bot is quit.
– Clicking on a user in the database will not delete that user.

To download Luxer, please look here: http://marcusfamily.info/~ryan/forums/index.php/topic,15.0.html
