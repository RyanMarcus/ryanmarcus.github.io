Luxer Update! Beta 4

1 Reply

I released a new version of Luxer, Beta #4.

Changes:
– Added a remove function to the built in user DB.
– Made the add/remove button cooler.
– Fixed an auto updater bug.
– Fixed the misspelling of Connect.
– Added a whoami command.
– After connection, Luxer will automatically switch to the chat tab.
– Added an option to notify you when the iTunes song changes.
– Fixed a bug when oping or designating yourself.

You can get the new version simply by reinstalling.

Last updated by Ryan Marcus at December 17, 2005.
